- (id) setupForDraw

Easy way to do rotations and translations if you inherit this class. Simply call: setLocation with your current location AND setRotation with your appropriate rotation Then you can call setupForDraw and based on location and rotation it will make gl calls to translate and rotate the current matrix

Make sure you call cleanUpAfterDraw once done drawing stuff
